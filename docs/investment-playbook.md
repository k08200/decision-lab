# Investment Playbook

Investment decisions should make sizing, valuation, catalysts, and break conditions explicit.

## Minimum Record

- asset and ticker
- proposed action
- time horizon
- portfolio role
- sizing rule
- base, bull, and bear case
- catalysts
- risk controls
- valuation or expected-return method
- what would change your mind

## Good Questions

- Is this a thesis decision, a sizing decision, or a timing decision?
- What is already priced in?
- What can break the thesis fastest?
- What is the opportunity cost versus holding cash or the benchmark?
- What drawdown would still feel acceptable?
- Which data would make the decision obviously wrong?

## Review Cadence

Review after:

- earnings
- major guidance change
- valuation model refresh
- thesis-breaking signal
- position-size drift
