# Review Loop

The review loop is where judgment compounds.

## Before The Decision

Write:

- expected signals
- failure signals
- review date
- success metrics
- what would change your mind

## After The Decision

Record:

- actual outcome
- which assumptions held
- which assumptions failed
- whether the selected option still looks rational using only information available at the time
- what to change in future decisions

## Avoid

- rewriting the original thesis after the outcome is known
- judging a good process only by a bad outcome
- judging a bad process only by a good outcome
- hiding low-confidence decisions that happened to work
