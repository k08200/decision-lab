# Business Decision Playbook

Business decisions should connect strategy, money, execution, and review cadence.

## Minimum Record

- strategic goal
- stakeholders
- constraints
- options
- financial impact
- execution owner
- smallest useful pilot
- dependencies
- kill criteria
- operating cadence

## Good Questions

- What is the default action if we do nothing?
- Is this reversible enough to pilot?
- Which stakeholder bears the cost?
- Which bottleneck does this actually remove?
- What would make us stop, reverse, or narrow the decision?
- What will we know in 30, 60, and 90 days?

## Review Cadence

Review weekly during pilots and monthly for ongoing operating decisions. Keep the review tied to the original criteria, not the story people prefer after the fact.
